How to Write a Simple Agreement Without a Lawyer

About this article

This article gives a plain-language framework for writing effective agreements without legal jargon or expensive drafting.

You do not always need complex legal language to create an effective agreement.

Start with:

  • Names of parties involved
  • Clear description of the work
  • Payment structure
  • Timeline
  • Signatures

Keep it simple, clear, and direct.
