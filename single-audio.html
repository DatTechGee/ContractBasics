Verbal vs Written Contracts: What You Need to Know

About this article

This article compares verbal and written contracts, focusing on proof, enforceability, and practical legal protection.

Verbal agreements can be legally valid in some cases, but they are difficult to prove.

Written contracts provide:

  • Clear evidence
  • Defined expectations
  • Legal protection

If it matters to you, put it in writing.
