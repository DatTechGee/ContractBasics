5 Common Mistakes People Make in Agreements

About this article

This article highlights five common agreement mistakes that lead to conflict and shows what to include for stronger, dispute-resistant contracts.

Even when people try to create agreements, they often make avoidable mistakes that lead to disputes.

  1. Not defining the scope clearly
  2. Leaving out payment details
  3. Ignoring timelines
  4. Not documenting changes
  5. Failing to get signatures

A good agreement is not just written, it is complete and precise.
