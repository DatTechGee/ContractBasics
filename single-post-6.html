Before You Sign: A 7-Point Contract Checklist

About this article

This article gives you a practical pre-signing checklist so you can catch common gaps before they become costly problems.

Before signing any agreement, review these seven essentials carefully:

  1. Confirm the full legal names of all parties
  2. Verify the scope of work is specific and measurable
  3. Check payment amount, method, and due dates
  4. Set start date, milestones, and completion deadline
  5. Define how revisions or changes will be approved
  6. Include what happens if either side defaults
  7. Ensure both parties sign and keep copies

A strong contract is not just signed, it is reviewed with clarity and intention.
